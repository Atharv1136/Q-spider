Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Paragraph:

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the founder of the Maratha Empire and one of the greatest warriors in Indian history. He was born on 19 February 1630 at Shivneri Fort. His mother, Jijabai, and his mentor, Dadoji Konddev, played an important role in shaping his character and leadership.

Shivaji Maharaj established Hindavi Swarajya, a kingdom based on justice, courage, and good governance. He was known for his brilliant military strategies, powerful navy, and respect for all religions. He captured many forts and protected his people from enemy attacks.

On 6 June 1674, he was crowned as Chhatrapati at Raigad Fort. His vision, bravery, and leadership continue to inspire millions of people even today.
