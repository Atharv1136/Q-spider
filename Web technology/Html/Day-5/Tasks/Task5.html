My Daily Routine

🌅 Morning (6:00 AM – 12:00 PM)

Wake up and freshen up.
Get ready for college.
Travel to college.
Attend lectures and practical sessions.
Revise notes during free time.

☀️ Afternoon (12:00 PM – 5:00 PM)

Have lunch.
Continue college lectures and lab sessions.
Work on assignments or college projects.
Return home.

🌇 Evening (5:00 PM – 9:00 PM)

Take some rest and have snacks.
Study programming and practice coding.
Work on personal projects or hackathon ideas.
Spend time learning new technologies.

🌙 Night (9:00 PM – 11:30 PM)

Have dinner with family.
Revise what I learned during the day.
Plan tasks for the next day.
Relax for a while and go to bed by 11:30 PM.
