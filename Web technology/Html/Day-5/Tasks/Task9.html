Making Tea ☕

Ingredients

To make a delicious cup of tea
you need water
milk
tea leaves
sugar
and ginger or cardamom (optional)
These ingredients give the tea a rich flavor and pleasant aroma.

Steps

First, boil the water in a pan.
Add the tea leaves and let them boil for a minute.
Next, add milk and sugar, then boil the mixture for 2–3 minutes.
If you like, add crushed ginger or cardamom for extra flavor.
Finally, strain the tea into a cup and serve it hot.
