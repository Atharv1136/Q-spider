India Successfully Launches New Weather Satellite

Reporter Name

By: Rahul Sharma

Published Date

20 June 2026

News Article

India has successfully launched a new weather satellite to improve weather
forecasting and disaster management. The satellite will help scientists monitor
cyclones, rainfall, and climate changes more accurately. Officials
said that this mission will benefit farmers, fishermen, and emergency services by providing timely weather updates.
The successful launch marks another important achievement in India's space program.
